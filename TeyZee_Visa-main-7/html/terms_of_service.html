How can I register at teyzeevisas.com as an authorized travel agent?

How do I recharge my account?
You can recharge your account using bank deposit, credit card, net banking, or payment at our branches by providing the admin code.

How can I set my own mark-up?
Using the mark-up field, you can set your own mark-up by a specific amount or value. Navigate to: My Account > My Profile > Mark-up/Transaction Fee (for all services ‚Äì Flight/Hotel/Bus, etc.).

What if the booking failed, but my account got debited?
Please contact our customer support team. A booking may have been generated at the supplier's end. Do not create another booking to avoid duplication.

How do I cancel tickets through the website?
Log in to the website, retrieve the booking from Itinerary, click on CANCEL Itinerary, select the specific passenger(s) or all passengers you wish to cancel, and send the request.

How do I get a refund for a cancelled itinerary?
The refund will be processed to your Teyzee Visas wallet account once the booking is cancelled. You can check the status under Wallet Account Status.

How do I get an account statement for tickets issued by us?
Go to the Account tab under My Account, where you can view statements date-wise (for a specific period as needed). You can also check the Sales Statement under the Sales tab, categorized by service (e.g., car).
